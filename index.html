What this is

A small, anonymous space to turn a swirling thought into words, receive a kind reflection, and be offered one tiny, doable grounding action you can try in the next 1–2 minutes.

What this isn’t

This does not replace therapy, crisis lines, or real-life support. It won’t tell you what decisions to make. It only aims to say: “You’re not strange for feeling this way, and here’s one small thing that might help your nervous system soften a bit.”

If you’re in real danger

If you’re thinking about harming yourself, feel unsafe, or are in an emergency, please contact local emergency services, a crisis hotline in your country, or someone you trust right now. You shouldn’t have to handle that alone.
